EVENTS

INDEPENDENCE DAY

We celebrate Independence Day with great patriotism, pride, and enthusiasm, typically around August 15th or nearby dates. The celebrations include flag hoisting ceremonies, singing of the National Anthem and National Pledge, and cultural programs featuring patriotic songs, dances, and speeches by students and staff. These events aim to kindle national pride and remind students of the spirit of freedom and the sacrifices made by the nation's heroes. Parents, teachers, and sometimes special guests join the gatherings, which foster a sense of unity and national responsibility among the school community.

TEACHERS DAY

WE celebrate Teachers’ Day with enthusiasm and respect to honor the dedication and hard work of their teachers. The celebrations typically include cultural programs, felicitation ceremonies, and speeches appreciating the role of teachers in shaping students' lives. For instance, Chinmaya Vidyalaya in Virugambakkam held a Teacher’s Day Felicitation Ceremony graced by school leaders and special guests, organized to pay tribute to teachers' immense contributions. These celebrations foster a warm atmosphere that highlights the importance of teaching and learning in the school community.

KRISHNA JANMASHTAMI

Chinmaya Vidyalayas celebrate Krishna Janmashtami with great joy, devotion, and a range of activities to honor the birth of Lord Krishna. The celebrations typically include decorating the school, organizing skits and plays recounting the childhood stories of Krishna, bhajans (devotional singing), and other cultural performances by students. For example, the kindergarten section at Chinmaya Vidyalaya Vaduthala was festively decorated, and students actively participated in celebrations that included storytelling and devotional songs. . The celebrations emphasize Krishna's qualities of love, wisdom, and righteousness,

SPORTS DAY

WE celebrate Sports Day with vibrant enthusiasm and a wide range of athletic activities. The events usually include track and field competitions, team sports, races, and various games, encouraging participation from students across all age groups. The Sports Day is marked by an opening march-past, followed by competitive and fun events demonstrating physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.For instance, Chinmaya Vidyalaya Vaduthala recently celebrated its 43rd Annual Sports Meet with great energy, featuring numerous competitions and performances.Tha annual sports day include awarding winners

ANNUAL DAY

The annual day at Chinmaya Vidyalaya Matriculation Higher Secondary School in Vadavalli, Coimbatore, is a celebration of student performances and achievements, often featuring various events and themes. For example, the 2025 annual day for junior grades had a "Coffee" theme, with students performing a play about the history of coffee. Recent annual events have been lauded for their organization and the talent displayed by students, with support from volunteers and teachers.

INVESTITURE DAY

The Investiture Day at Chinmaya Vidyalaya Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Vadavalli is an event to officially install newly elected student leaders, who are administered an oath, presented with badges, and motivated by a chief guest to take on their responsibilities. Recent ceremonies have featured local officials like Inspector of Police, Shri Ramesh Kumar K, and other esteemed guests who have guided the students to lead with integrity, compassion, and a spirit of service. The event is a formal occasion that marks the beginning of the student council's term, symbolizing their new roles and commitment to the school.

CHILDRENS DAY

Chinmaya Vidyalaya schools celebrate Children's Day through a variety of activities, such as teachers performing songs, dances, skits, and magic shows, and organizing student-led performances including poems, dances, and musical numbers. Other common celebrations include fun-filled games like Musical Chairs, storytelling and inspirational films, talent shows, and sometimes even educational events like science fairs.

KARTHIGAI DEEPAM

Chinmaya Vidyalaya schools celebrate Karthigai Deepam through various cultural activities such as students creating Rangoli with diyas, making eco-friendly lamps, and showcasing talents in music and dance. Some schools may also include prayers, chanting, and sharing videos about the festival and its significance.

MATHRU POOJA

To perform Mathru Pithru Pooja at a Chinmaya Vidyalaya school, students are guided through rituals that involve washing their parents' feet, applying vermilion, offering flowers, and chanting devotional songs to express gratitude. The ceremony typically begins with prayers and an explanation of the significance of the pooja, followed by the rituals, performances, and ending with blessings from parents.

SARASWATI POOJA

Saraswati Pooja at Chinmaya Vidyalaya schools typically involves placing books and tools of learning before an idol of Goddess Saraswati, followed by prayers and slokas offered by students and teachers. The celebration may include performances by students showcasing their artistic talents, prayers, and the distribution of prasadam (blessed food). Students wear traditional attire and are encouraged to perform a puja at the beginning of the day.

GAYATRI HOMAM

To perform a Gayatri Homam in a Chinmaya Vidyalaya school setting, follow the general guidance for such rituals which involves assembling at a designated spot for the havan, chanting the Gayatri mantra, and making offerings into the sacred fire. A teacher or priest will likely lead the ritual, which may also include a short discourse on the mantra, prayers, and the Vidyarthi Havan with five vows for success in education.

VISHNU SAHASRANAMA

To perform a Vishnu Sahasranama pooja at a Chinmaya Vidyalaya school, you should first ensure a clean environment and a calm, steady posture for chanting. Begin with a prayer to Lord Ganesha, chant the 108 names of Vishnu with devotion, and conclude with a prayer to Lord Hanuman. For a full puja, the process involves specific offerings, so contact the school or Chinmaya Mission for detailed guidance, as the exact procedures can vary.
