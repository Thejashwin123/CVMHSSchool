SENIOR SCHOOL

Chinmaya Vidyalaya Matriculation Higher Secondary School in Vadavalli, Coimbatore, is a co-educational school for grades 1-12, affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Board of Secondary Education (TNBSE). It emphasizes academic excellence with a focus on holistic development, incorporating spiritual and cultural values into its curriculum, according to Justdial and Skoodos. The school provides facilities like modern classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, and sports amenities, and follows an English medium of instruction.

School overview

Curriculum: Offers a Matriculation and higher secondary curriculum for grades 1 through 12.

Affiliation: Affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Board of Secondary Education (TNBSE).

Medium of instruction: English.

Establishment: Established in 1984, making it a long-standing institution in the area.

Management: Privately managed.

Facilities and environment

Infrastructure: Includes a private building, 18-21 classrooms, and a separate head teacher's room.

Amenities: Features well-equipped science and computer labs, a library with over 5,000 books, and a playground.

Safety: Uses CCTV surveillance in classrooms and has staff accompany students between blocks.

Health: Provides a medical room with a nurse and conducts health and hygiene sessions.

Educational approach

Holistic development: Focuses on the physical, mental, intellectual, and spiritual development of students.

Teaching philosophy: Aims to foster critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration through a supportive learning environment.

Parental involvement: Values parents as partners in education and encourages meetings with teachers.
