SPORTS

Chinmaya Vidyalaya in Vadavalli, Coimbatore has a strong sports program that emphasizes holistic development through a variety of indoor and outdoor games, including sports drills and cheerleading. The school has a large playground, courts for sports like basketball and throwball, and a play area with equipment like swings and sandpits. Sports are considered a vital part of the curriculum and contribute to the physical, mental, and emotional growth of students, with events like the annual Chinmaya Khel Milan held to promote overall well-being.

Indoor and outdoor games: The school offers a wide range of both indoor and outdoor sports to provide ample opportunities for participation and skill development.

Facilities: Students have access to a vast playground, a basketball court, a throwball court, and a dedicated play area with swings, seesaws, and sandpits.

Holistic development: Sports are an integral part of the school's commitment to the physical, mental, and emotional growth of students, as outlined in its educational philosophy.

Special events: The school hosts events like its annual Sports Day and participates in the inter-school Chinmaya Khel Milan to encourage overall well-being.

Record achievements: In 2019, the school set a record in the Tamilan Book of Records for having the greatest number of participants in a sports drill with cheerleading at a single venue.
