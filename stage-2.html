PRIMARY

Chinmaya Vidyalaya in Vadavalli, Coimbatore, offers primary education from Classes 1 to 8 in English for both boys and girls, with a separate pre-primary section. It is a private, co-educational school managed by the Chinmaya Mission, though there are two different schools in Vadavalli

Primary education details

Classes: The primary and upper primary section covers grades 1 to 8.

Medium of instruction: English is the medium of instruction.

School type: Co-educational.

Management: It is a private unaided school, managed by the Chinmaya Mission.
