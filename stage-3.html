HIGH SCHOOL

Chinmaya Vidyalaya Matriculation Higher Secondary School in Vadavalli, Coimbatore, is a private, co-educational school that follows the Tamil Nadu State Board curriculum, not CBSE. It was established in 1979 and offers classes from nursery to grade 12. The school has a playground, library, and various co-curricular activities and emphasizes value-based education in addition to academics.

Curriculum: Affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Board of Secondary Education.

Establishment: Founded in 1979.

Education focus: Provides education from nursery to Class 12.

Academics and holistic development: Follows the state board curriculum, with a strong emphasis on the overall development of students through co-curricular activities like sports, arts, and music.

Facilities: Includes classrooms, science and computer labs, a library with 5210 books, and sports facilities.

Management: A private unaided institution managed by the Chinmaya Mission group.

Language: English is the medium of instruction.
