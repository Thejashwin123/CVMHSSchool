PATRIOTISM

Chinmaya Vidyalaya in Coimbatore fosters patriotism through its Chinmaya Vision Programme, which integrates Indian culture and promotes patriotism alongside a universal outlook. This is achieved by focusing on the holistic development of students—physical, mental, intellectual, and spiritual—through various activities and the curriculum. Examples include events like a street play on freedom movements and embedding national pride in the overall learning environment.

Chinmaya Vision Programme: A foundational program designed to instill knowledge of Indian culture and a sense of patriotism, while also building a universal outlook.

Holistic development: The school aims for the integrated development of students' physical, mental, intellectual, and spiritual aspects, which is a cornerstone for developing well-rounded citizens.

Cultural integration: The program includes instilling a deep understanding of Indian culture to foster a sense of patriotism.

Examples in practice: The school has hosted events such as a street play on "Freedom Movements" to inspire and educate students about their country's history and heritage.
