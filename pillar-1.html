INTEGRATED DEVELOPMENT

Curriculum: Affiliated with CBSE, the school offers classes from LKG to Class 12 with various streams like PCM, PCB, and commerce.

Infrastructure: Facilities include well-equipped science, computer, and math labs, a library, and a multimedia room.

Focus: The CVP aims to enhance intellectual capacities through activities that promote creativity, independent thinking, and management skills.

Physical and emotional development

Physical Health: The school has a medical infirmary, a trained nurse, and conducts health and hygiene sessions. It also promotes physical fitness and sports.

Emotional and Social Health: The school provides a tranquil environment, conducts hobby classes, and focuses on emotional expansion, handling relationships, and mental health through counseling.

Infrastructure: Key infrastructure includes a spacious auditorium, and sports facilities.

Spiritual and cultural development

Vision: The CVP is inspired by Swami Chinmayananda, aiming to unfold students' physical, mental, intellectual, and spiritual levels.

Activities: This is achieved through value education, yoga, meditation, and other activities that help students discover their "inherent divinity".

Culture: The school aims to instill a strong sense of Indian culture and patriotism in its students.

Safety and environment

Campus: The school is located away from the city's congestion on a tranquil campus that is in harmony with nature.

Safety: The school ensures a safe and secure environment with measures like CCTV surveillance and monitors accompanying students between blocks.

Transport: A safe and disciplined bus service is available for pick-up and drop-off.
