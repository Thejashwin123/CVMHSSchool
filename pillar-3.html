UNIVERSAL OUTLOOK

The "Universal Outlook" is one of the four key tenets of the Chinmaya Vision Programme (CVP), a comprehensive educational framework used by Chinmaya Vidyalaya Coimbatore Matric Vadavalli to provide value-based, holistic education.

The universal outlook is based on the concept of "Vasudaiva Kudumbakam" ("One world, one family") and aims to foster a sense of global citizenship in students.

The specific aspects of the Universal Outlook are:

Global Citizenship: Helping the child view themselves as a responsible citizen of the world, sensitive and committed to universal issues like population and poverty.

Harmony with Creation: Encouraging students to live in harmony with nature through environmental education and sustainable practices, recognizing their role in preserving global peace and sustainability.

Awareness of Interconnectedness: Enabling students to become aware of being an intrinsic part of the macrocosm and understanding the unity and relationship between the universe, humanity, and the universal force behind creation.

Appreciation of Diversity: Fostering respect and empathy for diverse cultures, races, and beliefs worldwide, promoting tolerance and a global perspective.
