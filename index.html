about SCHOOL

CHINMAYA VIDYALAYA

Chinmaya School generally refers to the group of Chinmaya Vidyalayas operated by the Central Chinmaya Mission Trust (CCMT), founded by Swami Chinmayananda. These schools emphasize a balanced education integrating modern academic excellence with traditional spiritual and moral values. There are over 80 Chinmaya Vidyalayas across India and one in Trinidad. They offer a holistic curriculum designed to nurture well-rounded individuals, focusing on moral integrity, discipline, patriotism, and intellectual growth.

The first Chinmaya Vidyalaya started with a nursery school in Kollengode, Kerala in 1965 and has since expanded widely. The schools cater to diverse regions, from metropolitan cities to rural areas, with a commitment to inclusive education and community involvement. Chinmaya International Residential School (CIRS) in Coimbatore is a prominent example, offering CBSE and IB curricula on a large, scenic, and spiritually oriented campus.

EVENTS

INDEPENDENCE DAY

one of the awesome day

We celebrate Independence Day with great patriotism, pride, and enthusiasm, typically around August 15th or nearby dates. The celebrations include flag hoisting ceremonies, singing of the National Anthem and National Pledge, and cultural programs featuring patriotic songs, dances, and speeches by students and staff. These events aim to kindle national pride and remind students of the spirit of freedom and the sacrifices made by the nation's heroes. Parents, teachers, and sometimes special guests join the gatherings, which foster a sense of unity and national responsibility among the school community.

TEACHERS DAY

Respect to our teachers

WE celebrate Teachers’ Day with enthusiasm and respect to honor the dedication and hard work of their teachers. The celebrations typically include cultural programs, felicitation ceremonies, and speeches appreciating the role of teachers in shaping students' lives. For instance, Chinmaya Vidyalaya in Virugambakkam held a Teacher’s Day Felicitation Ceremony graced by school leaders and special guests, organized to pay tribute to teachers' immense contributions. These celebrations foster a warm atmosphere that highlights the importance of teaching and learning in the school community.

KRISHNA JANMASHTAMI

awesome festival

Chinmaya Vidyalayas celebrate Krishna Janmashtami with great joy, devotion, and a range of activities to honor the birth of Lord Krishna. The celebrations typically include decorating the school, organizing skits and plays recounting the childhood stories of Krishna, bhajans (devotional singing), and other cultural performances by students. For example, the kindergarten section at Chinmaya Vidyalaya Vaduthala was festively decorated, and students actively participated in celebrations that included storytelling and devotional songs. . The celebrations emphasize Krishna's qualities of love, wisdom, and righteousness,

Stages of school Eduction

KINDER GARDEN

Chinmaya Vidyalayas offer a well-structured Kindergarten program that serves as the foundational step in a child's educational journey. The program is designed to support holistic development, including physical, mental, intellectual, and spiritual aspects, following the vision of Swami Chinmayananda. The curriculum focuses on nurturing creativity, communication, social skills, cognitive growth, and emotional balance in a joyful and supportive environment.

VIEW

PRIMARY

Chinmaya Vidyalayas' primary education focuses on building a strong academic foundation while nurturing the overall development of young learners. The primary curriculum integrates core subjects such as languages, mathematics, science, and social studies with value-based education inspired by the Chinmaya Vision Programme (CVP). This holistic approach emphasizes intellectual growth, character development, emotional well-being, and cultural awareness.

VIEW

HIGH SCHOOL

Chinmaya Vidyalayas' high school education builds upon the strong foundational learning of the primary years, focusing on academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth. The curriculum usually follows the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) or state board guidelines, integrated with the Chinmaya Vision Programme (CVP) values. This stage emphasizes developing critical thinking, analytical skills, and conceptual clarity across subjects like mathematics, science, social studies, and languages.

VIEW

SENIOR SCHOOL

Chinmaya Vidyalayas' senior matriculation typically refers to the education offered in the senior secondary classes (11th and 12th) under the matriculation or state board system, depending on the specific school and region. This stream provides a curriculum structured to meet the requirements of the respective matriculation board, focusing on preparing students for state-level board examinations and further academic pursuits.

VIEW

SPORTS

Chinmaya Vidyalayas encourage sports participation at all educational levels, including in senior matriculation (senior secondary) classes. The schools emphasize physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship as integral parts of holistic education. Sports activities for senior students often include athletics, team games like basketball, volleyball, badminton, and traditional sports, fostering physical health alongside academic growth.
