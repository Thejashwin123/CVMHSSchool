INDIAN CULTURE

Chinmaya Vidyalaya Coimbatore Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Vadavalli, incorporates Indian culture through the Chinmaya Vision Programme (CVP), a comprehensive framework that integrates Indian values and philosophy with modern education. This program focuses on holistic development, cultural awareness, patriotism, and a universal outlook.

Value-Based and Spiritual Education: The school imparts value-based education and spiritual development through value education classes, daily mass chanting, meditation techniques, and yoga.

Appreciation of Heritage: Students are given exposure to the vast literary, artistic, and scientific heritage of India. This includes explanations of customs, traditions, and the symbolism behind them.

Scriptural Study: The curriculum includes the study of Hindu scriptures such as the Bhagavad Gita, the Vedas, the Ramayana, and the Mahabharata, adapted for different age groups.

Celebration of Festivals: The school celebrates various Indian festivals and organises cultural events and projects to foster cultural motivation.

Music and Arts: The school serves as a venue for major Indian classical music events, such as the "Coimbatore Margazhi Vaibhavam" Carnatic music festival, promoting traditional Indian arts.

"Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" (One World, One Family): The school promotes the Indian philosophical concept of "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam," which translates to "the world is one family," fostering a universal outlook and harmony with creation.

Emphasis on Sanskrit: The importance of the Sanskrit language is highlighted, with events like "Sanskrit Diwas" involving assemblies conducted entirely in Sanskrit.
