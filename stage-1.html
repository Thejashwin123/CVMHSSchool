KINDER GARDEN

Chinmaya Vidyalaya in Vadavalli offers kindergarten (LKG and UKG) as part of its matriculation school, focusing on the holistic development of young learners through a combination of modern and traditional approaches. The program aims to inspire children socially, physically, and mentally, with an environment that blends modern amenities with traditional values, including the Chinmaya Vision Programme. Key features include well-equipped classrooms, extracurricular activities like art and music, and safety measures such as CCTV surveillance.

About the kindergarten program

Focus: The program is designed for the holistic development of children, aiming to nurture their physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

Curriculum: The approach blends modern educational methods with traditional values, and the school uses technology like smart boards to facilitate learning.

Extracurriculars: Children are exposed to a variety of activities, such as music, dance, art and craft, and yoga.

School environment and infrastructure

Location: The school is situated away from the city's congestion on a tranquil campus.

Infrastructure: It includes well-equipped classrooms, a library, labs, and a dedicated kindergarten area.

Security: The school has implemented security measures, including CCTV surveillance in classrooms and procedures for student handovers.

Admission and parent partnership

Age requirement: Children must be 3 years and 6 months old as of April 1st to be considered for admission to LKG.

Interaction: Admissions involve an informal interaction with both the child and parents.

Parent involvement: The school considers parents as partners and allows them to meet teachers by appointment.

Additional details

Affiliation: The school is affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Board of Secondary Education (TNBSE).

Timings:

PRE KG: 9:30 AM to 12:31 PM (Monday to Friday).

LKG & UKG: 8:30 AM to 2:30 PM (Monday to Friday).
